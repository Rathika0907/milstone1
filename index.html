Internet access

To enhance access to information and communication, it is crucial to prioritize efforts aimed at providing widespread access to the internet. This involves deploying initiatives that bridge the digital divide and make online resources available to a broader population. Governments, NGOs, and private sector entities can collaborate to establish infrastructure, such as broadband networks, in underserved areas. Additionally, subsidies and incentive programs can be implemented to make internet-enabled devices more affordable.

  • Affordability Initiatives
